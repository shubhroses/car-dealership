About Us

Premium Auto Dealership has been serving our community with quality vehicles and exceptional service since 2005.

Our Story

At Premium Auto Dealership, we're more than just a car dealership. We're a family business committed to providing our customers with a transparent and enjoyable car buying experience.

Founded in 2005 by Shubhrose Singh, Premium Auto Dealership started as a small lot with just 10 pre-owned vehicles. Over the years, we've grown to become one of the most trusted dealerships in the region, offering a wide selection of new and pre-owned vehicles.

Our mission is simple: to help our customers find the perfect vehicle that meets their needs, budget, and lifestyle, while providing exceptional service throughout the entire process.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We believe in honesty and transparency in all our dealings. No hidden fees, no pressure tactics, just straightforward information to help you make an informed decision.

Quality

Every vehicle in our inventory undergoes a rigorous inspection to ensure it meets our high standards for quality, safety, and reliability.

Customer Focus

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to create a pressure-free, enjoyable experience that puts your needs first.
